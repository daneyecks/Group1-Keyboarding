Reference Line

This part is used if you want to refer to previous correspondence, account number or policy number of the person concerned. It serves as a guide for the receiver in locating the records in the files. REFERENCE LINE is typed in the same way you typed the SUBJECT LINE

reference : Account no. 559988

reference : Account policy number. 889955

Reference Initials

this is used as a guide to determine who composed and typed the letter. There are various ways of typing the reference initials.

- When the boss dictated the letter and transcribed by the secretary, type the initials of the boss in All Caps followed by the initials of the secretary in small letter seperated by a diagonal or colon .

Example: BOSS: NERIZA S. GUERRERO

SERCRETARY: JENNIFER D. GARCIA
